How Are Lab Diamonds Made..

Our lab created diamonds are grown from the tiny carbon
seeds of pre-existing diamonds.Advanced technology - either
extreme pressure and heat or a special deposition process
known as CVD - mimics the natural method of diamond forma-
tion.Some lab diamonds grown through deposition may also
undergo pressure and heat treatment after they are grown.
